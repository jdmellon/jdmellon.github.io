Morning Ritual

Objective

Design a branding and packaging system for a product of my choosing.

Solution

Greek yogurt packaging that breaks out of stereotypical imagery of Ancient Greek columns and urns by identifying a more specific target market. I chose to market my product to college aged women who are into activities such as going to museums, exploring nature, and staying fit.
