Recess

Objective

Develop Recess’ re-branding including general aesthetic, web site development, and business cards. Create client decks, poster designs, html emails, and social media ads.

Solution

Made a more cohesive visual language for the Recess brand through further identifying our target market. I used techniques such as moodboards and mind maps to determine key words, phrases, and imagery that is most connected to our market and longterm goals.
