Jack Hanna Pitch Deck

Objective

Create a contempory brand and pitch deck to send out to Jack Hanna’s potential partners and sponsors.

Solution

Moderist layouts inspired by National Geographic and Discovery. I juxtaposed this compositional foundation with fun and playful elements such as hexagonal patterns, gestalt effects, and cutout images superimposed on top of images to give a 3D effect.
